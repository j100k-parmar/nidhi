My World

Father

My father's name is Jayant Kulkarni. He is a linguistic expert. My relation with my father is like Krishna-Arjun relation. He always guides me and always shows me the right path. He is my mentor and always helps me in studies and clears my doubts. Even at times when he is angry with me, he explains me things very calmly. One thing I like about my father is that he is very patient. I love him a lot.

Mother

My mother's name is Dipika Kulkarni. She is a tuition teacher. She makes delicious food for me. She has taught me correct manners. She has given me very important life lessons, she is also the reason I am so hardworking and consistent to my work. She has always taught me that mind your words before you speak because few words can scar your relation with the person. She is the best friend I've ever had.

Family

Paternal Family: I have an uncle and an aunt. My uncle lives in Chandigarh and my aunt lives in Mumbai. We meet at specific occasions but we are connected to each other over voice calls and video calls. I have three cousins. Maternal Family: I have two uncles. Both of my uncles live near me, and we meet very often. I have lovely grandparents and I meet them regularly. I have two cousins.

Second Abode

I took admission in one of the prestigious schools in Vadodara, Prince Ashokraje Gaekwad school. It is located in Manjalpur. It was established by the Highness of Vadodara in the memory of her late husband, Prince Ashokraje Gaekwad (Grandson of King Sayajirao Gaekwad). I have been studying in the school since nursery and I have a very special bonding with the school, as it provides a very friendly and studious environment. Many religious functions are organized by our school, such as Rath Yatra, Guru Purnima, Christmas, etc. Along with the studies we get a lot of knowledge through various projects given to us. Teachers in this school are amazing, they always make the subject more interesting while teaching. Our school has two parts, one is till Senior Kindergarten, it is named after the queen of Vadodara, Rani Devyaniraje Gaekwad. And the other part is from 1st standard till 12th standard and is named after the late Prince, Prince Ashokraje Gaekwad. We also have lot of sporting facilities such as, basketball, volleyball, gymnastic, etc. Our school provides affordable education. It concentrates on the holistic development of a child. Link of School Website: http://www.pargs.org/

Hobbies

Reading Books

Whenever I am free I love to read novels, encyclopedias and other books. I have read a few novels such as Faster Fenay, Hamlet, Murder on the Orient Express and Sacrifice. My favorite authors are Enid Blyton and Agatha Christie. I like novels because we can learn a lot from them, like: Our Increase in Vocabulary. We learn new words when we read them. We do not repeat mistakes. If we read a biography of a person, we learn from the mistake of the person in biography and we do not repeat it in our life.

Drawing Pictures

I like to draw pictures in my free time. Drawing increases our imagination. It helps me understand myself better. Drawing has given me a lot of fame and name because I have won a prize in drawing competition. Ancient people used to draw and convey the situations. One of my favorite painters is Raja Ravi Varma.

Hearing Songs

I love listening songs. Music is a therapy, it can heal anyone. It provides me with a good company. One advantage of music ids that there is song for every mood and situation. I hear songs of 70s as well as modern. My favorite singers are Kishore Kumar, Alka Yagnik, Arijit Singh and Neha Kakkar. The apps that I use for listening songs are Gaana and Saavn.

Watching Movies

I also like watching dramas and movies, but I am very choosy and selective in watching movies. I watch movies which provide us with some values and teachings. I think that watching these kind of movies provide lessons to upcoming generations. For example movies like 3 Idiots convey us that do what your heart wants, not something under pressure of someone and many other philosophical teachings. But I do not watch movies often.

Activities

  • I have anchored and given many speeches in school on special occasions and festivals.
  • I participate in all extracurricular activities in school.
  • Every Saturday I went for aerobics.
  • I went for skating & rope malkhamb classes when I was a kid.
  • I completed my second level of drawing in Reet Mehendi Drawing Classes.
  • I also learnt Western dance at the age of 7.
  • I have also participated in sports like lawn tennis and football.

Achievements

  • I belong to Cauvery house in my school. Competitions are held in our school every year and I have won first position in the few of them such as Science Quiz, and English Debate.
  • I have completed my first year in Kathak and third year in Bharatnatyam.
  • I was a player of our Football School Team which participated in Under 17 Inter School Football Competition.
  • I have received a Certificate of Distinguished Performance from Asset Tests.
  • I have also achieved first position in 5th standard in Silverzone Olympiad and 95 percentile in 6th standard in Silverzone Olympiad.

My Friends

My friends are my lifeline. They are very selfless. We all have a great bond. They always support me. They always give me honest opinions and help me out in every situation. I share everything with them with no fear that they will judge me. I spend at least half an hour a day with my friends. They are always there to cheer me up and to boost my confidence level. I have many friends in school but the special one is Janki and in society also I have many friends but the special ones are Vidhi, Krishna and Swara. They always provide a moral support to me. Due to corona I and Janki do not meet very often but we meet at least once a month and we talk to each other over mobile phone. She companies me in almost everything. We fight with each other daily but we also unite again at the end of the day because we know that we cannot live without each other. My friends mean everything to me.

Education

पढाई एक इंसान के लिए बहुत महत्त्वपूर्ण है. मुझे पढाई इसलिए अछि लगाती है क्योंकि उससे हमारा ज्ञान बढ़ता है और हमारा आत्मविश्वास भी बढ़ता है. हमें ज्ञान हर तरफ से मिलता है सिर्फ उसे पहचान कर हमें उसे बटोरना चाहिए. मै पाठशाला में भी हर पाठ्येतर गतिसिधि में भाग लेती हु क्योंकि मेरा मानना है की हम उसमे से भी बहुत कुछ सीख सकते है जैसे की जब हम कोई समूह खेल खेलते है तब हम सबके साथ मिलके काम करना सीखते है, और हम बहुत कुछ सीख सकते है. किसी महान पुरुष ने कहा है की, "काबिल होने के लिए पढो, फिर कामयाबी ज़रूर तुम्हारा साथ देगी". ए. पी. जे. अब्दुल कलाम ने कहा है, "अगर तुम सूरज की तरह चमकना चाहते हो तो सूरज की तरह जलना भी सीखो".

Mobile/Cell Phone Profile Messages Settings
As we all know that mobile means a lot to us. A person literally cannot live without mobile nowadays. Mobile has a lot of advantages such as we can talk to a person who is far away in other country, state or city, we can listen to songs anytime anywhere, we can find information about anyone, anything literally. We can chat with our friends, family, etc. Mobiles have become an inextricable part of our lives. Even while dining with our family we use phones or even when we hang out with friends everyone is busy in their phones, some updating status, some clicking pictures, etc. But due to these mobile phones we have forgotten to live the moments. We can definitely use mobiles again but we cannot bring those moments back. We don't realize that using mobiles without having any limits is harming us. The rays from the mobile can also cause brain tumor, it also affects a person's sleep. Nowadays when a child or even an infant starts crying the parent gives him/her mobile so that it stops crying. But the rays which mobiles emit can have adverse effects on the child. Most importantly we have become mobile addicts. We have lost the control on ourselves. People become so engrossed in their mobiles that they do not even have time to look in their surroundings. When we go on a holiday or a trip for a break from our hectic lives, we do not give break to our usage of mobiles. Even there we start using mobile instead of enjoying the moment. We can easily chat with someone who is far away from us but we do not even look at the person sitting beside us. Also because of phones we have forgotten to spend quality time with our family. From my perspective we have become slaves of mobile phones. We must learn to utilize it in a proper way so that we can get good benefit out of it and at the same time we can live in the present life.
